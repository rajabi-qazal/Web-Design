Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.This classic omlette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Prepartion time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preoaration:5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillimgs:chesse,diced vegtables,coocked meat,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl,beat the eegs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed.You can add tablespoon of water or milk for a fliffier texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tiilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add filling (optimal):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen fikkings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continuse to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the fillings.Let it cook for another minute,then slide it anoto a platre.
  6. Enjoy:Servve hot,with additional salt and pepper if needed.
